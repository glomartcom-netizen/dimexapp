About DimeX

DimeX is a blockchain-based staking ecosystem built on BNB Smart Chain. The project focuses on transparency, security, and sustainable reward distribution using verified smart contracts.

Our platform allows users to stake DX tokens and earn rewards through a decentralized on-chain system designed for long-term growth and community participation.
